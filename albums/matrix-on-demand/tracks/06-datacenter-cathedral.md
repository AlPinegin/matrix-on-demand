# 06. Datacenter Cathedral

## Discovery

The new cathedrals are datacenters built to compute human context at scale.

## Suno Style

Epic industrial synthwave, huge drums, choir-like pads, low male vocal,
cathedral reverb, 84 BPM, dark cinematic atmosphere, massive final chorus.
