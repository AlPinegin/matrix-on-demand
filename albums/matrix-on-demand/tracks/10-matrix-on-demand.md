# 10. Matrix On Demand

## Discovery

The album reveals that every previous technology was a stage in assembling one
machine: personalized reality.

## Suno Style

Finale track, cinematic cyber-rock, slow ominous intro into enormous chorus,
choir pads, analog synth bass, distorted guitars, 88 BPM, apocalyptic but
beautiful, final outro loops back to track 1.
