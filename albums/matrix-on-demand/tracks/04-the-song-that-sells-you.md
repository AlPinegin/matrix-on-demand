# 04. The Song That Sells You

## Discovery

Advertising disappears into music that knows the listener too well.

## Suno Style

Sleek cyberpop, infectious chorus, expensive synths, soft trap drums, seductive
but sinister vocal, 105 BPM, radio-ready hook with dark undertone, big final
chorus.
