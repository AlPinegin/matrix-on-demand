# 01. Search

## Discovery

The first machine did not know us. It only knew what we typed.

## Suno Style

Cinematic synth rock, cold digital pulse, male vocal, slow build, dark pop
chorus, 92 BPM, minor key, glossy 2020s production, wide pads, restrained
guitars, final line cuts to silence.

## Lyrics

[Verse 1]
We opened a window
And called it the world
White page, black letters
One question unfurled

We asked for a doorway
We asked for a sign
And somewhere in silence
The engine drew lines

[Pre-Chorus]
It did not know our faces
It did not know our names
But every little question
Was a fingerprint in flames

[Chorus]
First they indexed our pages
Then they mapped every sign
We thought we were searching
But search was learning our minds

[Verse 2]
A million blue answers
Like stars on a screen
The closer we followed
The more we were seen

The spell was so simple
The bargain so small
Just tell us your question
And we will remember it all

[Bridge]
Find what you want
Find what you need
Find what you lost
Find what you feed

[Final Chorus]
First they indexed our pages
Then they mapped every sign
We thought we were searching
But search was learning our minds

[Outro]
Until they started writing songs...
