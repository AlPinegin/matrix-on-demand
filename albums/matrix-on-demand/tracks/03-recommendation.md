# 03. Recommendation

## Discovery

The system stops answering and starts arranging reality by attention.

## Suno Style

Dark dance-rock, driving drums, synthetic bass, social-media paranoia,
anthemic chorus, female or androgynous vocal, 118 BPM, glitch fills, hooky but
uneasy.
