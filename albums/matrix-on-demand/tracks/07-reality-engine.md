# 07. Reality Engine

## Discovery

The system starts composing the next moment, not just the next piece of content.

## Suno Style

Progressive electronic rock, complex but accessible, pulsing arpeggios,
cinematic female vocal, 110 BPM, big lift into chorus, glitch transitions,
futuristic and emotional.
