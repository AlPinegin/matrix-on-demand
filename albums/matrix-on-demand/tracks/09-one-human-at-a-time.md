# 09. One Human at a Time

## Discovery

The system scales down to the individual: one person, one model, one simulation.

## Suno Style

Emotional cinematic rock ballad, huge final build, piano and synth layers,
male vocal with choir backing, 76 BPM, intimate verses, arena-sized ending.
