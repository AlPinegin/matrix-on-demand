# 05. NPC No More

## Discovery

Characters stop being scripted and begin carrying user context.

## Suno Style

Cinematic industrial pop-rock, game soundtrack energy, heavy drums, distorted
synth bass, theatrical spoken lines, male/female duet possible, 96 BPM,
dramatic chorus.
