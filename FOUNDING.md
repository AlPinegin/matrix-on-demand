# Founding

July 4, 2026.

The project did not begin with a song.

It began with a question:

> Where does personalization end?

The answer slowly emerged:

> Search became recommendation.
> Recommendation became advertising.
> Advertising became music.
> Music became experience.
> Experience became reality.

The working phrase was **Matrix On Demand**.

Not one Matrix for everyone, but billions of personal worlds, each rendered
around a single human context.

The idea came from a progression:

- search engines learned what people wanted;
- advertising learned what people would buy;
- recommendation systems learned what people would watch;
- generative systems learned what people would feel;
- reality engines may learn what each person should experience next.

The accidental dictation phrase "on divan" became useful too: the old couch was
where a person watched the screen; the new couch watches the person. Objects,
music, NPCs, feeds, and rooms become interfaces for computation.

This is the first record of the project.

The repository exists to preserve the evolution of the idea, not only the final
lyrics.
